# Example: Customer Support / Customer Success

## Example Input (condensed)
JD highlights:
- Role: Customer Support Lead / CSM
- Outcomes: Reduce backlog, improve CSAT/NPS, build escalation workflows, maintain KB
- Must-haves: Ticketing tools (Zendesk/Intercom), escalation processes, KB management

Base Resume (condensed):
- Tier 2 Support; owned macros, built KB articles, triaged bugs, led weekly triage with Eng; no direct reports.

## What the Skill Should Detect
- Must-haves: ticket triage/escalation, KB management, CSAT, tooling
- Strengths: macros, KB, bug triage, cross-functional cadence
- Gaps: formal people leadership

## Narrative Choice
- Position as Support Ops/Lead-ready IC with measurable process improvements
- Emphasize backlog reduction, CSAT improvements, and tooling best practices
- Clarify lead scope via initiatives, not title inflation

## Output Excerpt (Tailored Bullets)
- Reduced median first response from 9h → 3h by redesigning queues, introducing macros, and clarifying escalation paths; maintained >92% CSAT over 3 quarters.
- Authored 40+ knowledge base articles and established review standards, cutting repetitive how-to tickets by ~18%.
- Ran weekly triage with Product/Eng, improving time-to-escalation by 35% and eliminating duplicate tickets across teams.

## Rationale Notes
- Coverage: backlog reduction, CSAT improvements, KB governance, escalation workflows
- Gap handling: no people management claims; initiative-led influence showcased
- Trade-offs: downplayed unrelated responsibilities to foreground process and outcomes
